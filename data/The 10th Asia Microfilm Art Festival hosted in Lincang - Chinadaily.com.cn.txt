The 10th Asia Microfilm Art Festival closed in Lincang, Yunnan province, with its annual gala on Nov 26.
Representatives and experts of film and cultural organizations, including Zhao Huayong, honorary president of China Television Artists Association, and Yao Yonghui, general manager of China Central Newsreel and Documentary Film Studio (Group), joined the gala and presented certificates to award winners.
Eight awards were given out at the gala, including the most popular performer award received by Lyu Wei. Yu Xiaoguang was presented with the best actor award, while Hu Caihong and Tao Huimin were given the best actress awards.
